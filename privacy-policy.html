1.What is Privacy Policy?

The Privacy Policy of Loyal Health is a crucial document that outlines how we handle and protect your personal information. It defines the principles and guidelines we follow to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of the data you provide to us. In this section, we provide an overview of our Privacy Policy, emphasizing the importance of protecting your privacy rights. We understand the significance of privacy and strive to maintain the highest standards of data protection. Our Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, the purposes for which we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your data. It also explains your rights and choices regarding the information you share with us. By clearly explaining our Privacy Policy, we aim to instill confidence in our visitors and patients that their personal information is handled responsibly and in accordance with applicable privacy laws. We encourage you to review our complete Privacy Policy to gain a comprehensive understanding of how we handle your data and ensure your privacy is respected.

2.To whom does this Privacy Policy apply?

This Privacy Policy applies to all individuals who visit our website or engage with our healthcare services at Loyal Health. We are dedicated to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of personal information collected from visitors, patients, and any other individuals who interact with us.


This Privacy Policy applies to the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data by Loyal Health. It encompasses all channels through which we collect information, including our website, patient registration process, contact forms, and any other means by which personal data is shared with us. Whether you are a visitor exploring our website, a patient seeking our healthcare services, or an individual contacting us through our online forms, this Privacy Policy governs how we handle your personal information.

We are committed to ensuring that your personal data is handled with care and in compliance with applicable privacy laws. By accessing our website or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data as outlined herein. Please note that this Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites or services that may be linked from our website. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those external websites before providing any personal information. By extending the coverage of this Privacy Policy to all individuals who interact with Loyal Health, we aim to provide transparency and clarity regarding the handling of personal information and reaffirm our commitment to protecting your privacy rights.

3.What personal data do we process?

  • Patient Registration Information: We collect and store personal details like name, contact information, date of birth, and medical history to provide tailored healthcare services and ensure continuity of care.
  • Contact Information: When visitors reach out to us via contact forms, email, or phone, we may collect their name, email address, and phone number to address inquiries and maintain effective communication.
  • Website Analytics: We use website analytics tools to gather data on browsing behavior (e.g., IP addresses, browser type) for website analysis, improving user experience, and enhancing our services.
  • Consent and Data Security: We obtain explicit consent before collecting personal data and implement robust security measures to protect it from unauthorized access.
  • Data Retention: We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill purposes, comply with legal obligations, or address ongoing medical requirements. Once no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.
  • Sharing of Personal Data: Personal data is not disclosed to third parties without explicit consent, except when required by law or to provide healthcare services (e.g., sharing information with medical professionals involved in treatment).
